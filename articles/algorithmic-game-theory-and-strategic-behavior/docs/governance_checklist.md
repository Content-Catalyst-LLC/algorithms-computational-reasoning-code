# Governance Checklist: Algorithmic Game Theory and Strategic Behavior

- Are all strategic agents identified?
- Are action spaces and reporting pathways documented?
- Are payoff assumptions explicit and reviewed?
- Is the mechanism documented clearly enough for audit?
- Does the mechanism align incentives with desired behavior?
- Were manipulation, gaming, and collusion pathways tested?
- Were incentive and equilibrium sensitivity analyses performed?
- Were welfare, fairness, and distributional effects reviewed?
- Is post-deployment adaptation monitored?
- Can rule changes, strategic responses, and impacts be reconstructed?
