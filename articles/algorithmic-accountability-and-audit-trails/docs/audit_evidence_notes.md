# Audit Evidence Notes

Audit trails are only useful when evidence is complete, timely, consistent, protected, and accessible.

Required evidence may include:

- use-case approval records;
- data provenance and dataset versions;
- feature registries and label definitions;
- model cards and model versions;
- threshold and configuration records;
- validation and fairness reports;
- decision logs and reason codes;
- human review and override notes;
- appeal and correction records;
- incident reports and remediation actions;
- monitoring and drift records;
- retirement or rollback records.

If a decision cannot be reconstructed, accountability is weakened.
