# Use Boundaries

This companion folder is educational. It is not legal advice, compliance certification, audit certification, civil-rights advice, model-risk approval, medical advice, credit advice, public-benefits advice, employment advice, or operational guidance.

Do not use the synthetic accountability scores here to certify real systems, approve deployment, or claim legal or regulatory compliance.

Before using accountability and audit-trail metrics in practice, require:

- legal and governance review;
- domain expertise;
- validated audit requirements;
- role-specific accountability ownership;
- privacy, security, and retention review;
- contestability and remediation pathways;
- independent audit where warranted.
