# Method Notes

This folder treats accountability as an operational and institutional capacity.

The core method is:

1. Define the decision, stakes, affected people, and accountable owner.
2. Map required audit records across data, model, evaluation, deployment, decision, review, appeal, incident, and remediation layers.
3. Score audit completeness and evidence quality.
4. Score accountability capacity across documentation, provenance, reviewability, contestability, remediation, and governance ownership.
5. Estimate decision reconstruction risk.
6. Identify systems requiring review or escalation.
7. Connect findings to monitoring, appeal pathways, remediation, and governance change.

The examples are dependency-light and educational.
