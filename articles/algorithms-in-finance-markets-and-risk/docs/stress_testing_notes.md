# Stress Testing Notes

Financial stress testing should examine:

- market shocks;
- credit shocks;
- liquidity shocks;
- funding stress;
- margin calls;
- counterparty failures;
- cyber or operational outages;
- crowded trades;
- volatility jumps;
- correlation breakdown;
- model failure;
- reverse stress scenarios.

Stress tests are useful when they reveal fragility and change decisions.
