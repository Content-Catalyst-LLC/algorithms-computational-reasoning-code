# Use Boundaries

This companion folder is educational. It is not investment advice, trading advice, credit advice, lending advice, insurance advice, consumer-finance advice, tax advice, accounting advice, legal advice, regulatory compliance advice, market-risk certification, model validation certification, or operational guidance.

Do not use the synthetic scores here to approve real financial systems, make investment decisions, deny credit, execute trades, price loans, manage portfolios, certify model risk, or claim regulatory compliance.

Before using financial algorithm metrics in practice, require:

- qualified financial expertise;
- legal and compliance review;
- model validation;
- consumer-protection review where relevant;
- market-risk and liquidity review where relevant;
- independent audit where warranted;
- monitoring, incident response, and stop authority.
