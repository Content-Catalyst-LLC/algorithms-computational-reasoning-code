# Model Risk Notes

Model risk is the risk of adverse consequences from incorrect, misused, poorly governed, or misunderstood models.

Common sources include:

- weak conceptual design;
- poor data quality;
- stale data;
- coding errors;
- overfitting;
- distribution shift;
- bad assumptions;
- weak calibration;
- inappropriate deployment scope;
- undocumented changes;
- excessive reliance;
- no monitoring;
- no stop rule.

Model risk governance should include inventory, validation, monitoring, challenge, audit trail, and lifecycle retirement.
