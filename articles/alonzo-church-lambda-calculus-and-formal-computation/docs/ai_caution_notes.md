# AI Caution Notes

Church's formal tradition clarifies a distinction between apparent reasoning and formal reasoning.

Useful questions:

- What is the syntax?
- What is the semantics?
- What counts as a valid reduction or inference step?
- What is being represented?
- What can be checked by types, proofs, tests, or specifications?
- What cannot be decided by a general procedure?
- Who is responsible for design and deployment?

AI belongs in the toolkit, not in control.
