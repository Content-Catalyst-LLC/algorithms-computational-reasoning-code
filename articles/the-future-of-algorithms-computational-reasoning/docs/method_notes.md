# Method Notes

This folder treats future algorithm review as a readiness problem.

The core method is:

1. Define the problem and purpose.
2. Identify the algorithmic role: support, recommendation, automation, or control.
3. Document data, provenance, and knowledge architecture.
4. Assess uncertainty, failure modes, and distribution shift.
5. Evaluate human judgment requirements.
6. Test contestability and explanation.
7. Assess governance capacity.
8. Decide whether to deploy, delay, redesign, limit, or refuse.

The goal is to match technical capability with governance maturity, human judgment, evidence discipline, contestability, monitoring, and repair.
