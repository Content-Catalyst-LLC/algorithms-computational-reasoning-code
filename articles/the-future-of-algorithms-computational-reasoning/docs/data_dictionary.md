# Data Dictionary

## future_algorithmic_domains.csv

- `domain_id`: future algorithmic domain.
- `technical_capability`: current or plausible technical strength.
- `institutional_consequence`: consequence for rights, access, safety, attention, resources, or public trust.
- `uncertainty`: uncertainty in data, model, environment, or outcome.
- `automation_level`: degree of delegated action.
- `opacity`: difficulty of understanding or explaining behavior.
- `contestability_need`: need for notice, reason, appeal, and correction.
- `governance_maturity`: maturity of documentation, audit, oversight, and monitoring.
- `human_judgment_requirement`: need for human authority and domain interpretation.
- `failure_severity`: severity of plausible failure.
- `deployment_readiness`: readiness for responsible deployment.

## future_algorithmic_systems_map.csv

- `risk_score`: interpretive average of consequence, uncertainty, automation, opacity, contestability, judgment, and failure severity.
- `readiness_score`: interpretive average of technical capability, governance maturity, and deployment readiness.
- `future_status`: review category.

## future_algorithmic_cautions.csv

- `caution`: risk to avoid.
- `meaning`: explanation of the caution.
