# Contestability and Appeal Review

Contestability review should ask whether workers and applicants can:

- receive notice that algorithms are used;
- understand the decision role of the system;
- receive reasons for consequential outcomes;
- correct inaccurate data;
- provide context;
- reach a qualified human reviewer;
- appeal decisions;
- receive timely remedy where warranted.

High-stakes workplace automation requires notice, explanation, review, correction, documentation, and appeal.
