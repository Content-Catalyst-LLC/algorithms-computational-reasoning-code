# Governance Review

Workplace algorithm governance should include:

- algorithm inventory;
- labor-impact assessment;
- fairness and opportunity review;
- privacy and surveillance review;
- contestability and appeal process;
- human review and worker voice;
- safety review;
- procurement and vendor accountability;
- monitoring;
- audit trails;
- incident response;
- stop, redesign, remediation, and retirement authority.

Organizational accountability requires knowing which systems exercise power and how that power is governed.
