# Fairness and Opportunity Review

Workplace fairness review should examine:

- applicant flow;
- hiring criteria validity;
- subgroup selection rates;
- error rates;
- rating bias;
- customer bias;
- workload distribution;
- schedule stability;
- promotion pathways;
- pay and incentive distribution;
- disability accommodations;
- caregiving constraints;
- geographic access;
- appeal outcomes.

Workplace equity requires examining how algorithmic systems distribute opportunity, not only how they process data.
