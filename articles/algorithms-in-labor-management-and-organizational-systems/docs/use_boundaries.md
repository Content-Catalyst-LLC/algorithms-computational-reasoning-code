# Use Boundaries

This companion folder is educational. It is not legal advice, employment advice, human-resources compliance advice, labor-relations advice, workplace-safety certification, hiring validation certification, privacy compliance certification, or operational guidance.

Do not use the synthetic scores here to approve real workplace systems, automate hiring, discipline workers, deactivate workers, set pay, certify workplace privacy, or claim employment-law compliance.

Before using workplace algorithm metrics in practice, require:

- qualified human-resources and labor expertise;
- legal and privacy review;
- worker-safety review;
- fairness and opportunity review;
- worker voice and contestability;
- independent validation where warranted;
- monitoring, remediation, and stop authority.
