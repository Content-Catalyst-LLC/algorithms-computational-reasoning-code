# Threshold Notes

Thresholds convert scores into action.

A threshold should document:

- the score or probability being thresholded;
- the action triggered;
- false-positive consequences;
- false-negative consequences;
- affected people or institutions;
- equity and subgroup effects;
- review and override conditions;
- appeal or contestability path;
- monitoring signals;
- revision history;
- stop or retirement rules.

Thresholds are policy choices expressed as numbers.
