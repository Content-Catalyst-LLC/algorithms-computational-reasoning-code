# Environmental Justice Review

Environmental justice review should examine:

- unequal exposure;
- historical underinvestment;
- monitoring and sensor gaps;
- service quality differences;
- affordability burden;
- project benefit distribution;
- hazard vulnerability;
- community priorities;
- participation and contestability;
- public health and environmental impacts.

Infrastructure algorithms should help institutions see unequal risk, not convert it into neutral-looking scores.
