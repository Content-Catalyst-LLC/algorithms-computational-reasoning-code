# Use Boundaries

This companion folder is educational. It is not engineering advice, infrastructure safety certification, climate-risk certification, utility operations advice, emergency-management advice, investment advice, legal advice, environmental compliance advice, regulatory compliance advice, or operational guidance.

Do not use the synthetic scores here to approve real infrastructure systems, operate grids, dispatch emergency resources, certify resilience, allocate public funds, issue hazard warnings, or claim regulatory compliance.

Before using infrastructure algorithm metrics in practice, require:

- domain engineering expertise;
- climate and environmental expertise where relevant;
- legal and regulatory review;
- environmental justice review;
- public-sector governance review where relevant;
- independent validation where warranted;
- monitoring, emergency response, and stop authority.
