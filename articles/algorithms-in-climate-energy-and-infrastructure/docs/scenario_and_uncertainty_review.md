# Scenario and Uncertainty Review

Climate, energy, and infrastructure algorithms should document:

- time horizon;
- spatial resolution;
- baseline assumptions;
- climate or hazard scenario;
- demand scenario;
- technology scenario;
- cost assumptions;
- physical constraints;
- omitted effects;
- uncertainty bands;
- model boundary conditions;
- adaptive decision points.

Uncertainty should be a design input, not a footnote.
