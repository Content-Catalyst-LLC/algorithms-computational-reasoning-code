# EDVAC and Credit Notes

The phrase "von Neumann architecture" is useful but historically incomplete.

Careful framing:

- von Neumann's EDVAC draft helped disseminate and standardize architectural vocabulary;
- ENIAC and EDVAC work involved Mauchly, Eckert, Goldstine, Burks, and many others;
- architecture emerges through reports, engineering work, institutional circulation, and implementation;
- publication and naming are not the same as sole invention.

Use von Neumann as shorthand only with collaborative context.
