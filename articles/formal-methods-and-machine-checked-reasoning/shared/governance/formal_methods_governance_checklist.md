# Formal Methods Governance Checklist

- State the system boundary and verification scope.
- Write the formal specification.
- Document assumptions and environmental conditions.
- Identify preconditions, postconditions, invariants, and variants.
- Generate and preserve proof obligations.
- Record proof assistant, model checker, solver, or analyzer outputs.
- Preserve counterexamples and failed obligations.
- Distinguish proved, checked, bounded, unknown, failed, and out-of-scope claims.
- Review model-scope and specification fit with domain experts.
- Maintain lifecycle governance as code, assumptions, and deployment conditions change.
