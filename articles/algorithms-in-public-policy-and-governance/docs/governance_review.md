# Governance Review

Algorithmic public governance should include:

- algorithm inventory;
- legal authority review;
- impact assessment;
- data provenance and proxy review;
- model documentation;
- due-process plan;
- human review protocol;
- appeal and remedy pathway;
- procurement and vendor accountability;
- monitoring and audit plan;
- public reporting where appropriate;
- stop, rollback, and retirement authority.

Public algorithms should make government more accountable, not harder to challenge.
