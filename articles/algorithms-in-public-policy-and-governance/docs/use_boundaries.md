# Use Boundaries

This companion folder is educational. It is not legal advice, compliance certification, civil-rights advice, administrative-law advice, public-sector procurement advice, public-benefits advice, fraud-investigation advice, or operational guidance.

Do not use the synthetic scores here to certify public-sector systems, approve deployments, deny benefits, target enforcement, investigate fraud, or claim regulatory compliance.

Before using public algorithmic governance metrics in practice, require:

- legal review;
- civil-rights and due-process review;
- domain expertise;
- affected-person and stakeholder input where appropriate;
- procurement and vendor review;
- independent audit where warranted;
- monitoring, remedy, and stop authority.
