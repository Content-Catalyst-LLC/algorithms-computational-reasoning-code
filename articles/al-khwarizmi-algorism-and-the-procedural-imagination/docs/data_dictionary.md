# Data Dictionary

## procedural_legacy_themes.csv

- `theme_id`: interpretive theme in al-Khwārizmī's procedural legacy.
- `procedure`: degree to which the theme involves clear stepwise method.
- `representation`: importance of numerals, prose, tables, problem classes, or other representational systems.
- `transmission`: importance in movement across languages, teaching traditions, or later reception.
- `application`: degree of practical use in arithmetic, algebra, astronomy, commerce, administration, or education.
- `modern_resonance`: degree to which the theme clarifies modern computational reasoning.

## al_khwarizmi_procedural_legacy_map.csv

- `legacy_score`: average of the theme dimensions.
- `interpretive_status`: core, major, or supporting procedural legacy.

## interpretation_cautions.csv

- `caution`: interpretive risk to avoid.
- `meaning`: explanation of the caution.

## procedural_legacy_summary.csv

- `themes_reviewed`: number of legacy themes reviewed.
- `core_legacy_threads`: themes marked as core procedural legacy.
- `major_legacy_threads`: themes marked as major procedural legacy.
- `supporting_legacy_threads`: themes marked as supporting procedural legacy.
- `mean_legacy_score`: mean interpretive legacy score.
