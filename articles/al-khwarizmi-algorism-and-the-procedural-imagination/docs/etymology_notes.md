# Etymology Notes

The modern word algorithm is connected through medieval Latin forms of al-Khwārizmī's name. This is a major historical clue, but it should not be treated as proof that all algorithmic procedure began with one person.

Use this distinction:

- Al-Khwārizmī is central to algorithmic naming and the history of algorism.
- Algorism is tied to written calculation with Hindu-Arabic numerals.
- Algorithm later generalizes toward finite, rule-governed procedure.
- Procedural reasoning has multiple ancient and medieval lineages.

Etymology is important, but etymology is not the whole history.
