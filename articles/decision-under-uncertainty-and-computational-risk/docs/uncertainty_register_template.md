# Uncertainty Register Template

| Item | Description | Owner | Review question | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Probability estimate | How likely is the adverse outcome? | Modeling team | Is the estimate calibrated and current? | draft |
| Utility function | How are benefit, harm, and cost weighted? | Decision owner | Whose values are represented? | draft |
| Action threshold | When does the system recommend action? | Governance board | Is the threshold justified and contestable? | draft |
| Error cost | What happens when the system is wrong? | Risk owner | Who bears the burden of error? | draft |
| Missing evidence | What important uncertainty is not measured? | Review team | What cannot be inferred from available data? | draft |
| Monitoring plan | How will drift be detected? | Operations owner | What triggers review or shutdown? | draft |
