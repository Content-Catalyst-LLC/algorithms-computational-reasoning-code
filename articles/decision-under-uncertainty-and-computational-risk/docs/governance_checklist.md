# Governance Checklist

Use this checklist before treating a computational risk recommendation as decision support.

| Review area | Question | Evidence to inspect |
|---|---|---|
| Decision scope | What decision is being supported? | Decision statement and use boundary. |
| Option set | Were relevant alternatives included? | Options table and exclusion notes. |
| Probability quality | Are risk estimates calibrated and current? | Validation report and calibration checks. |
| Utility assumptions | Who defined benefit, cost, and harm? | Utility register and stakeholder review. |
| Threshold legitimacy | Why is this action threshold justified? | Threshold policy and escalation rule. |
| Distributional effects | Who bears false positives, false negatives, and costs? | Subgroup and impact analysis. |
| Sensitivity | Does the recommendation change under plausible assumptions? | Sensitivity grid and robustness report. |
| Contestability | Can affected people challenge the decision? | Appeal pathway and human-review policy. |
| Monitoring | How will drift and failure be detected? | Monitoring plan and audit trail. |
