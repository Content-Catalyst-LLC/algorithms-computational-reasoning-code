# Use Boundaries

This companion repository is for education, article support, and reproducible demonstration only.

Do not use these scripts to make or automate real decisions about people, health, credit, employment, insurance, public benefits, safety, law enforcement, education, housing, or other consequential settings.

A real decision-under-uncertainty system requires domain validation, legal review, ethical review, calibration testing, affected-stakeholder engagement, monitoring, escalation rules, appeal mechanisms, and clear institutional responsibility.
