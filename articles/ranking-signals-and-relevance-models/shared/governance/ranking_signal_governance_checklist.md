# Ranking Signal Governance Checklist

- Define the ranking purpose and user task.
- Inventory lexical, field, metadata, authority, freshness, semantic, behavioral, and governance signals.
- Document how signals are weighted and combined.
- Evaluate top results and buried relevant results.
- Compare ranking quality across categories, topics, source types, and user tasks.
- Review click and behavioral feedback for position bias and popularity reinforcement.
- Separate freshness from authority unless the query is time-sensitive.
- Preserve provenance, source metadata, snippets, and explanation evidence.
- Audit diversity, coverage, and near-duplicate result concentration.
- Provide a correction path for misleading or harmful ranking behavior.
