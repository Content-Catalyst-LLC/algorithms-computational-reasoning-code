# Governance Review

Automation-bias governance should include:

- clear human authority statements;
- uncertainty and limitation displays;
- reliance and acceptance monitoring;
- override and disagreement logs;
- review-time and workload monitoring;
- alert fatigue review;
- appeal and correction pathways;
- training and competency records;
- accountability assignments;
- periodic oversight audits.

Human oversight should be demonstrated through behavior, not merely promised in policy.
