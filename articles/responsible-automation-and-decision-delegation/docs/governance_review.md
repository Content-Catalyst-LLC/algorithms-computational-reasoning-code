# Governance Review

Responsible automation governance should include:

- use-case legitimacy review;
- automation-level selection;
- delegation boundary documentation;
- evidence and validation review;
- stakes and reversibility assessment;
- escalation triggers;
- human review requirements;
- notice, explanation, appeal, and correction pathways;
- monitoring and incident response;
- rollback and retirement authority.

Governance should make it easier to constrain automation than to expand it without review.
