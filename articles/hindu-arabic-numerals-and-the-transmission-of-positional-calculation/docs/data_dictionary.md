# Data Dictionary

## positional_calculation_themes.csv

- `theme_id`: interpretive theme in positional calculation and numeral transmission.
- `representation`: importance of symbolic structure, place value, zero, and compact encoding.
- `procedure`: degree to which the theme involves repeatable arithmetic operations.
- `transmission`: importance in movement across languages, texts, scripts, and institutions.
- `practical_use`: degree of use in commerce, astronomy, administration, education, and accounting.
- `pedagogy`: importance of teaching, written rules, copied examples, and curriculum.
- `modern_resonance`: degree to which the theme clarifies modern computational representation.

## positional_calculation_transmission_map.csv

- `positional_score`: average of the theme dimensions.
- `interpretive_status`: core, major, or supporting positional-calculation thread.

## interpretation_cautions.csv

- `caution`: interpretive risk to avoid.
- `meaning`: explanation of the caution.

## positional_calculation_summary.csv

- `themes_reviewed`: number of positional calculation themes reviewed.
- `core_threads`: themes marked as core positional calculation threads.
- `major_threads`: themes marked as major positional calculation threads.
- `supporting_threads`: themes marked as supporting positional calculation threads.
- `mean_positional_score`: mean interpretive positional score.
