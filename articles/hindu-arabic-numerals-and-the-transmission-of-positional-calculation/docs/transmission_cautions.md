# Transmission Cautions

Avoid these claims:

- Hindu-Arabic numerals were invented only by Arabs.
- Indian origins are enough to explain global transmission.
- Zero is a trivial placeholder.
- Digit shapes are the whole story.
- Notation is superficial.
- Transmission is passive copying.

Prefer these claims:

- The decimal positional system developed in Indian mathematical contexts.
- Arabic and Islamic-world scholarship adapted, taught, named, and transmitted the numerals and procedures.
- Latin reception, algorism texts, commercial practice, and education transformed adoption.
- Place value, zero, and written arithmetic changed what calculation could become.
