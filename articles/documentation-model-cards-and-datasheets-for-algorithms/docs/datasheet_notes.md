# Datasheet Notes

A datasheet should document:

- dataset motivation and creators;
- collection process, source systems, consent and licensing conditions;
- population coverage, sampling boundaries, and missingness;
- features, labels, annotation rules, and known ambiguity;
- preprocessing, filtering, transformations, aggregation, and cleaning;
- recommended, discouraged, and prohibited uses;
- maintenance, correction, retention, access, and distribution rules;
- known representational, measurement, privacy, and governance risks.

A datasheet makes the dataset's history visible enough to question.
