# Governance Review

Attention-system governance should include:

- algorithm inventory;
- objective and metric review;
- attention impact assessment;
- recommendation guardrails;
- moderation governance;
- monetization and creator-impact review;
- advertising and targeting review;
- transparency and contestability mechanisms;
- user-control review;
- experiment governance;
- monitoring and incident response;
- stop, rollback, friction, and redesign authority.

A platform that governs attention should also govern its own incentives.
