# Use Boundaries

This companion folder is educational. It is not legal advice, platform compliance advice, moderation policy advice, advertising advice, psychological advice, safety certification, or operational guidance.

Do not use the synthetic scores here to certify real platforms, approve ranking systems, moderate real content, set ad policies, classify users, or claim compliance.

Before using attention-system metrics in practice, require:

- platform governance review;
- legal and policy review;
- user research where appropriate;
- creator and community impact review;
- privacy and safety review;
- independent audit where warranted;
- monitoring, appeal, remedy, rollback, and stop authority.
