# Moderation Governance Notes

A moderation governance record should include:

- policy category;
- classifier or rule used;
- threshold values;
- confidence score interpretation;
- human review route;
- appeal path;
- restoration or remedy process;
- false-positive and false-negative monitoring;
- language and context limitations;
- creator and community impact;
- policy-change history.

Moderation governance should preserve context, contestability, and repair.
