# Clocks and Timed State Notes

Al-Jazarī’s clocks are important because they convert material change into visible timed events.

Important interpretive ideas:

- water level can act as state;
- controlled flow can create timing;
- floats can register internal condition;
- displays, sounds, and figure movements can mark events;
- reset conditions enable repeated cycles;
- clock behavior depends on calibration and material constraints.

A clock is a machine for making time procedural.
