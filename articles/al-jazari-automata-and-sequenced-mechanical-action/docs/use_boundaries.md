# Use Boundaries

This companion folder is educational and interpretive. It is not engineering, construction, safety, or operational guidance.

Use these files to support:

- teaching historical automata and mechanical reasoning;
- conceptual mapping;
- analysis of sequenced action in material systems;
- structured comparison of automata themes;
- computational scaffolds for humanities-oriented analysis.

Do not use the calculators or examples to build, operate, certify, or repair real mechanical, hydraulic, pneumatic, pressure-based, clockwork, safety-critical, or water-raising devices.
