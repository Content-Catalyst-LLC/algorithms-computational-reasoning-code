# Privacy and Data Governance Notes

Health algorithm privacy review should include:

- data minimization;
- purpose limitation;
- access controls;
- audit logs;
- security controls;
- secondary-use review;
- de-identification and re-identification risk;
- retention and deletion rules;
- data-sharing boundaries;
- patient, public, or community trust considerations where appropriate.

Health data governance should protect people while allowing responsible learning and public benefit.
