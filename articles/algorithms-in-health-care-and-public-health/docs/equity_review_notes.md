# Equity Review Notes

Health algorithm equity review should examine:

- who is included and excluded from data;
- missingness patterns;
- access barriers;
- label quality;
- proxy variables such as cost, utilization, or documentation;
- subgroup performance;
- false-positive and false-negative burden;
- clinical actionability across groups;
- language, disability, geography, and socioeconomic context;
- whether prediction produces beneficial intervention.

A health algorithm is equitable only if its benefits and burdens are evaluated in the care system where it operates.
