# Use Boundaries

This companion folder is educational. It is not medical advice, clinical advice, diagnostic advice, treatment advice, public-health advice, legal advice, privacy advice, regulatory compliance advice, health-equity certification, clinical validation certification, or operational guidance.

Do not use the synthetic scores here to approve real health algorithms, make clinical decisions, triage patients, diagnose disease, allocate care, deny services, conduct surveillance, or claim regulatory compliance.

Before using health algorithm metrics in practice, require:

- qualified clinical expertise;
- public-health expertise where relevant;
- legal and privacy review;
- clinical validation;
- health-equity review;
- workflow and safety review;
- independent audit where warranted;
- monitoring, incident response, and stop authority.
