# AI Caution Notes

Turing's machine-intelligence work should be read carefully.

Useful cautions:

- the imitation game reframes thinking behaviorally;
- behavior is evidence, not a complete theory of mind;
- fluency is not the same as grounding;
- computability does not equal feasibility;
- AI systems require evaluation, governance, and human judgment.

Turing's legacy supports AI ambition and AI caution together.
