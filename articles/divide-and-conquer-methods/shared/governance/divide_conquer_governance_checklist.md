# Divide-and-Conquer Governance Checklist

- Define the original problem and intended whole.
- Document the partition rule and why it preserves meaning.
- Define base cases, progress measures, and stopping conditions.
- Verify subproblem validity and recursive-call contracts.
- Specify combination or aggregation logic.
- Test odd sizes, empty inputs, duplicates, unbalanced partitions, and boundary effects.
- Analyze recurrence, recursion depth, space cost, and recombination cost.
- Preserve partition IDs, partial outputs, run manifests, and merge/aggregation records.
- Review whether decomposition hides context, relationships, or accountability.
