# Ethics and Boundaries

Cryptanalysis has legitimate historical, educational, defensive, and scholarly uses. It can also be misused.

This folder is limited to:

- historical interpretation;
- toy classical ciphers;
- synthetic examples;
- educational frequency counting;
- safe discussion of limits and countermeasures.

Do not use this material for unauthorized access, privacy invasion, intrusion, credential theft, real-world surveillance, or evading modern security systems.
