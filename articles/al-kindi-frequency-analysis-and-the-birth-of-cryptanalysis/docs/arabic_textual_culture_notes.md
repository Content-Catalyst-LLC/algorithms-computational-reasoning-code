# Arabic Textual Culture Notes

Al-Kindī’s method should be situated in a rich textual environment involving grammar, lexicography, rhetoric, Qur’anic study, poetry, administration, translation, mathematics, and philosophy.

Frequency analysis depends on language knowledge:

- common letters;
- common words;
- grammatical structures;
- genre expectations;
- spelling conventions;
- repeated particles and endings.

Cryptanalysis is both quantitative and philological.
