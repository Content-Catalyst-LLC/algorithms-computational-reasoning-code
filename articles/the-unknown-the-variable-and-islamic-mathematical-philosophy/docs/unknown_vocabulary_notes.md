# Unknown Vocabulary Notes

Important terms and roles:

- thing: a named sought quantity;
- root: base unknown quantity;
- square: power or area-like representation of the root;
- number: known quantity or constant;
- case: recognized algebraic form;
- rule: procedure attached to the case;
- result: recovered value;
- verification: check against the original problem.

This vocabulary made algebraic reasoning possible before modern symbolic notation became standard.
