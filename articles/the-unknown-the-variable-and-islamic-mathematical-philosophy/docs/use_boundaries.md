# Use Boundaries

This companion folder is educational and interpretive.

Use these files to support:

- teaching the historical unknown and variable thinking;
- conceptual mapping of Arabic algebra and Islamic mathematical philosophy;
- analysis of verbal procedure, case classification, demonstration, and translation;
- structured comparison of unknown-variable themes;
- computational scaffolds for humanities-oriented analysis.

Do not treat the synthetic scores as manuscript facts or as a replacement for specialist philological, codicological, paleographic, mathematical, or historical analysis.
