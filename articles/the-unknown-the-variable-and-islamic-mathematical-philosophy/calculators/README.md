# Calculators

These small calculators support article concepts without external packages. They are educational conceptual scaffolds, not historical proof tools.

## Python calculators

- `unknown_score_calculator.py` — average unknown-variable interpretive dimensions.
- `equation_case_classifier.py` — classify a simple algebraic case involving square, root, and number.
- `square_completion_calculator.py` — compute the completion term for x^2 + bx = c.
- `solution_verification_calculator.py` — verify whether a candidate satisfies x^2 + bx = c.
- `translation_continuity_calculator.py` — score continuity across Arabic, Latin, vernacular, and symbolic forms.
- `variable_transition_calculator.py` — classify a concept as unknown, named unknown, symbolic unknown, variable-like, or parameter-like.
- `origin_story_caution_calculator.py` — flag anachronistic origin-story language.

## R calculators

- `unknown_variable_score.R` — compute unknown-variable philosophy score.
