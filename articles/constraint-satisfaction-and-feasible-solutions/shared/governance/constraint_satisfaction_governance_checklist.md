# Constraint Satisfaction and Feasible Solutions Governance Checklist

- Define variables and explain why each variable belongs in the model.
- Document domains and the source of allowable values.
- Inventory all hard constraints, soft constraints, policy constraints, fairness constraints, and exception rules.
- Define feasibility clearly: what counts as a valid solution?
- Preserve violation records for infeasible assignments.
- Explain pruning, propagation, and backtracking decisions.
- Identify overconstrained systems and conflicting rules.
- Provide exception, override, review, and appeal pathways where decisions affect people.
- Audit domain exclusions and hidden constraints.
- Evaluate distributional effects of rules and feasible-set design.
