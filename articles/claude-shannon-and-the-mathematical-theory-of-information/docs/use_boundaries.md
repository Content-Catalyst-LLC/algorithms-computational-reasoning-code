# Use Boundaries

This companion folder is educational and interpretive.

Use these files to support:

- teaching Shannon information, entropy, bits, coding, compression, channel capacity, and error correction;
- explaining the distinction between information and meaning;
- connecting information theory to algorithms, networks, cryptography, AI, and data governance;
- building simple calculators and smoke tests for article concepts.

Do not treat synthetic scores as channel-capacity proofs, cryptographic guarantees, compression benchmarks, AI safety guarantees, or governance decisions.
