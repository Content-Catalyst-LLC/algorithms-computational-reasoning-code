# AI and Information Governance Notes

AI systems are also information systems.

Ask:

- what is collected?
- how is it encoded?
- what gets compressed or abstracted?
- what uncertainty is modeled?
- what noise and bias enter the pipeline?
- what outputs are transmitted to users or institutions?
- what information leaks?
- what is meaningful beyond the measured information quantity?
- who is accountable for acting on outputs?

AI belongs in the toolkit, not in control.
