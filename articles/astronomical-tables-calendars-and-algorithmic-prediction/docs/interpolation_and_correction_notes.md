# Interpolation and Correction Notes

Astronomical tables often require values not explicitly listed.

Key procedures:

- lookup: find the nearest or exact entry;
- interpolation: estimate between two entries;
- correction: adjust a mean or approximate value;
- rounding: choose a usable precision;
- comparison: check against observation or another table;
- revision: update a model, table, or parameter.

These procedures show that prediction is structured expectation under assumptions, not certainty.
