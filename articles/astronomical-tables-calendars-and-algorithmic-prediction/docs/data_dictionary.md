# Data Dictionary

## astronomical_prediction_themes.csv

- `theme_id`: interpretive theme in astronomical tables and algorithmic prediction.
- `table_structure`: importance of rows, columns, arguments, entries, and stored values.
- `procedural_clarity`: degree to which the theme involves ordered use-rules.
- `predictive_function`: degree to which the theme produces future-oriented or time-dependent results.
- `institutional_use`: importance in ritual, administration, calendar practice, navigation, agriculture, or education.
- `correction_awareness`: importance of interpolation, correction, error, approximation, and revision.
- `transmission_importance`: importance of copying, commentary, translation, teaching, and localization.
- `modern_resonance`: degree to which the theme clarifies modern predictive computation.

## astronomical_prediction_map.csv

- `prediction_score`: average of the theme dimensions.
- `interpretive_status`: core, major, or supporting astronomical prediction thread.

## interpretation_cautions.csv

- `caution`: interpretive risk to avoid.
- `meaning`: explanation of the caution.

## astronomical_prediction_summary.csv

- `themes_reviewed`: number of prediction themes reviewed.
- `core_threads`: themes marked as core astronomical prediction threads.
- `major_threads`: themes marked as major astronomical prediction threads.
- `supporting_threads`: themes marked as supporting astronomical prediction threads.
- `mean_prediction_score`: mean interpretive prediction score.
