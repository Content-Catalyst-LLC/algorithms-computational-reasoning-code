# Al-Khwārizmī Without Myth

- His name is central to the word history of algorithm through algorism.
- His works helped systematize and transmit calculation and algebraic procedures.
- He is central to the history of arithmetic, algebra, and procedural reasoning.
- He did not invent every algorithm.
- He was not a modern computer scientist.
- His work belongs to a broader Islamic-world, Arabic, Persianate, Abbasid, Indian, and Latin transmission history.

Precision honors him better than myth.
