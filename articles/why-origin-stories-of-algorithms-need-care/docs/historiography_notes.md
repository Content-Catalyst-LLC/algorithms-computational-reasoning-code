# Historiography Notes

Historiography is the study of how history is written.

For algorithm origin stories, ask:

- What kind of origin is being claimed?
- Which evidence supports the claim?
- Which categories are modern?
- Which traditions and actors are included?
- Which are omitted?
- Is the story about a word, a practice, a concept, or a formal theory?
- Is an analogy being used as a literal identity?

A careful origin story multiplies beginnings responsibly.
