# Data Dictionary

## origin_story_themes.csv

- `theme_id`: interpretive theme in the origin-story care map.
- `evidence_grounding`: degree to which claims depend on named evidence rather than slogan.
- `scope_clarity`: degree to which the story distinguishes word, concept, practice, institution, and modern definition.
- `anachronism_control`: degree to which modern categories are not projected backward uncritically.
- `network_awareness`: degree to which the story includes translators, scribes, teachers, users, institutions, and traditions.
- `etymology_caution`: degree to which word history is not confused with invention.
- `transmission_depth`: degree to which reception, translation, copying, teaching, and institutional memory are included.
- `credit_distribution`: degree to which credit is spread across relevant actors and traditions.
- `public_usefulness`: degree to which the story remains teachable and memorable.
- `historical_significance`: importance for the history of algorithmic reasoning.
- `modern_resonance`: degree to which the theme clarifies AI-era algorithm narratives today.

## origin_story_care_map.csv

- `origin_care_score`: average of the theme dimensions.
- `interpretive_status`: core, major, or supporting origin-story care thread.

## interpretation_cautions.csv

- `caution`: interpretive risk to avoid.
- `meaning`: explanation of the caution.
