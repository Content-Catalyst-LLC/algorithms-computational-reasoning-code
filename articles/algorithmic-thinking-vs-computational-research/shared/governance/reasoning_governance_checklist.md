# Reasoning Governance Checklist

- Separate the real-world concern from the computational task.
- Document the representation and what it omits.
- Explain the procedure, stopping conditions, and edge cases.
- Record correctness tests and validation limits.
- Estimate resource use and scaling behavior.
- Explain how outputs should and should not be interpreted.
- Identify who is affected by error or misuse.
- Define monitoring, appeal, revision, and retirement conditions.
