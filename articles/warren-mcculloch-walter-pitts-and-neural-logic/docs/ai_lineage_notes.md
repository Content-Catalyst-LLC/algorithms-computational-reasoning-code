# AI Lineage Notes

McCulloch and Pitts belong in AI history because they helped make networks of simple units thinkable as computational systems.

The lineage connects to:

- cybernetics;
- automata theory;
- perceptrons;
- connectionism;
- symbolic AI debates;
- neural networks;
- hybrid AI;
- representation learning;
- responsible AI abstraction boundaries.

They should not be described as inventors of modern deep learning. Their model is a foundational abstraction, not a full account of current AI architectures.
