# Data Dictionary

## neural_logic_concepts.csv

- `concept_id`: neural-logic concept.
- `logical_clarity`: clarity of formal logical relation.
- `neural_abstraction`: relevance to simplified neural modeling.
- `computational_relevance`: connection to computation and algorithms.
- `cybernetic_connection`: relation to feedback, signals, and control.
- `ai_lineage`: relevance to later AI and neural-network history.
- `biological_caution`: need to avoid overstating biological realism.
- `historical_influence`: influence on later fields.
- `interpretability`: how clearly the concept can be explained.
- `formal_tractability`: ease of formal or computational treatment.
- `responsible_use_relevance`: value for responsible AI interpretation.

## mcculloch_pitts_neural_logic_map.csv

- `neural_logic_score`: average concept score.
- `interpretive_status`: core, major, or supporting neural-logic thread.

## threshold_logic_examples.csv

- `gate`: simplified logical operation.
- `x1`, `x2`: binary inputs.
- `threshold`: firing threshold.
- `output`: binary output.
