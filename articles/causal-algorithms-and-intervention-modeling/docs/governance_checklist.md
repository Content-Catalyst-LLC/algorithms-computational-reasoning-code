# Governance Checklist for Intervention Models

## Causal design

- Is the intervention clearly defined?
- Is the comparison condition explicit?
- Are confounders listed and reviewed?
- Is the adjustment set justified?
- Are mediators separated from confounders?
- Is the time order valid?

## Implementation

- Is the intervention feasible in the institutional setting?
- Are resources, costs, staffing, and operational constraints documented?
- Are risks and unintended consequences reviewed?
- Are distributional effects checked across groups or contexts?

## Accountability

- Who owns the decision to use the intervention model?
- Who can challenge assumptions or outcome definitions?
- Are results presented as conditional rather than universal?
- Is the model prohibited from being used outside its scope?

## Communication

- Are causal assumptions visible to nontechnical reviewers?
- Are sensitivity results summarized plainly?
- Are uncertainty and failure modes documented?
