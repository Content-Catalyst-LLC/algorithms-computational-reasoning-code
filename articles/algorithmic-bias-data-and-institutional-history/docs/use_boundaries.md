# Use Boundaries

This companion folder is educational. It is not legal, civil-rights, compliance, employment, credit, health, public-benefits, housing, insurance, policing, education, platform-governance, or operational advice.

Do not use the synthetic bias-history scores here to certify real systems, approve deployment, make high-stakes decisions, or claim legal compliance.

Before using bias and institutional-history metrics in practice, require:

- legal and civil-rights review;
- domain expertise;
- affected-person and stakeholder consultation;
- validated data and provenance review;
- label and proxy review;
- documented metric choices and tradeoffs;
- contestability and remediation pathways;
- independent audit where appropriate.
