# Method Notes

This workflow distinguishes the construct a system claims to learn from the features and labels it actually observes.

The synthetic generator intentionally creates three teaching problems:

1. **Proxy substitution** — observable variables stand in for harder-to-measure social or institutional concepts.
2. **Patterned missingness** — absence of data is related to institutional access rather than random chance.
3. **Label noise** — the observed label partially reflects prior administrative decisions rather than the underlying construct.

The audit does not claim to solve measurement politics. It creates a reproducible structure for documenting where measurement choices enter machine-learning workflows.
