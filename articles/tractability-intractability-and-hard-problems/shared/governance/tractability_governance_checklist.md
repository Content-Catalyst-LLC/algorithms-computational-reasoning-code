# Tractability Governance Checklist

- Define whether the problem is decision, search, optimization, counting, or online.
- Define input-size dimensions and relevant parameters.
- State known complexity evidence or uncertainty.
- Identify helpful structure, restrictions, or special cases.
- Benchmark exact methods and record scale thresholds.
- Evaluate approximation, heuristics, randomization, relaxation, and reformulation alternatives.
- Define timeout behavior and fallback procedures.
- Validate approximate or heuristic solution quality.
- Preserve certificates, witnesses, solver logs, and audit records when possible.
- Communicate what is exact, approximate, heuristic, unknown, or infeasible.
