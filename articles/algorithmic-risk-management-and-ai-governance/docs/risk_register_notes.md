# Risk Register Notes

A risk register should connect risk concerns to concrete controls and owners.

Required fields may include:

- risk identifier;
- system and use case;
- affected people or institutions;
- risk description;
- severity;
- likelihood;
- detectability;
- uncertainty;
- current controls;
- control owner;
- monitoring signal;
- escalation threshold;
- remediation plan;
- status;
- review date.

A risk register is not useful when risks have no owners, no evidence, no thresholds, and no consequences.
