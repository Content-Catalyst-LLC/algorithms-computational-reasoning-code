# Governance Review

Algorithmic risk management and AI governance should include:

- system inventory;
- use-case approval;
- impact assessment;
- risk and control registers;
- model and data documentation;
- validation and monitoring;
- human review and escalation;
- incident response;
- appeal and correction;
- vendor governance;
- pause, rollback, and retirement authority.

Governance should be evaluated by what it can change, not only by what it documents.
