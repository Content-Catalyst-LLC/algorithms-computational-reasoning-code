# Method Notes

This folder treats AI governance as lifecycle risk management.

The core method is:

1. Inventory the proposed or active algorithmic system.
2. Classify use case, stakes, affected people, delegated authority, and institutional purpose.
3. Identify risk items and failure modes.
4. Score severity, likelihood, detectability, governance readiness, and control effectiveness.
5. Estimate inherent risk, residual risk, and escalation status.
6. Assign owners, controls, monitoring signals, and remediation actions.
7. Review the system continuously through monitoring, appeals, incidents, audits, changes, and retirement.

The examples are dependency-light and educational.
