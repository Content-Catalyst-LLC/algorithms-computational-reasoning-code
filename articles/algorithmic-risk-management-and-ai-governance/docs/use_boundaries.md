# Use Boundaries

This companion folder is educational. It is not legal advice, compliance certification, audit certification, risk approval, civil-rights advice, medical advice, credit advice, public-benefits advice, employment advice, or operational guidance.

Do not use the synthetic governance scores here to certify real systems, approve deployment, or claim regulatory compliance.

Before using algorithmic risk governance metrics in practice, require:

- legal and governance review;
- domain expertise;
- risk management expertise;
- affected-person and stakeholder input where appropriate;
- privacy, security, and safety review;
- validated monitoring and evaluation methods;
- contestability and remediation pathways;
- independent audit where warranted.
