# Method Notes

This folder treats al-jabr wa'l-muqabalah as rule-governed algebraic procedure.

The core method is:

1. Identify the problem statement and practical context.
2. Determine known quantities, unknown quantities, and relations.
3. Classify the equation or problem type.
4. Identify restoration, balancing, completion, reduction, or comparison operations.
5. Track what remains invariant through transformation.
6. Examine geometric, verbal, or numerical demonstration.
7. Check how examples teach the general method.
8. Connect the procedure to modern computational reasoning without projecting modern symbols backward.

The examples are dependency-light and educational.
