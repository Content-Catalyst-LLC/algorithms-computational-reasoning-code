# Data Dictionary

## algebraic_procedure_themes.csv

- `theme_id`: interpretive theme in rule-governed algebraic procedure.
- `classification`: degree to which the theme organizes problems into types.
- `transformation`: degree to which the theme involves valid problem-state transformation.
- `representation`: importance of words, unknowns, diagrams, quantities, or notation.
- `demonstration`: importance of correctness, proof, or geometric/verbal justification.
- `practical_use`: degree of connection to commerce, inheritance, surveying, education, or practical calculation.
- `transmission`: importance in teaching, translation, and reception.
- `modern_resonance`: degree to which the theme clarifies modern computational reasoning.

## rule_governed_algebra_map.csv

- `procedure_score`: average of the theme dimensions.
- `interpretive_status`: core, major, or supporting rule-governed algebra thread.

## interpretation_cautions.csv

- `caution`: interpretive risk to avoid.
- `meaning`: explanation of the caution.

## algebraic_procedure_summary.csv

- `themes_reviewed`: number of algebraic procedure themes reviewed.
- `core_threads`: themes marked as core rule-governed algebra threads.
- `major_threads`: themes marked as major rule-governed algebra threads.
- `supporting_threads`: themes marked as supporting rule-governed algebra threads.
- `mean_procedure_score`: mean interpretive procedure score.
