# Origin Story Cautions

Avoid these claims:

- Algebra began in one book.
- Al-jabr is only a word origin.
- Early algebra was primitive modern algebra.
- Medieval verbal procedure is inferior because it lacks modern symbols.
- Islamic-world mathematics merely preserved earlier knowledge.
- Procedure equals programming.

Prefer these claims:

- Al-Khwārizmī's treatise is a major systematizing and naming moment.
- Al-jabr wa'l-muqabalah matters as procedural transformation.
- Early algebra should be studied in its verbal, geometric, practical, and pedagogical context.
- Rule-governed algebra is a major root of computational reasoning.
