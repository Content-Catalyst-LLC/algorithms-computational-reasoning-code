# Data Dictionary

## reception_themes.csv

- `theme_id`: interpretive theme in algorism, algebra, and reception.
- `procedural_portability`: importance of a method becoming teachable and executable in a new setting.
- `notation_change`: importance of numerals, place value, zero, symbols, layout, and representation.
- `translation_pathway`: importance of Arabic-Latin, Hebrew, vernacular, school, or merchant transfer routes.
- `teaching_value`: importance of worked examples, manuals, classroom use, and pedagogy.
- `practical_utility`: importance for commerce, accounting, conversion, surveying, inheritance, or astronomy.
- `institutional_adoption`: importance of schools, universities, merchant houses, notaries, or administrations.
- `trust_verification`: importance of checking, auditability, public trust, and resistance.
- `historical_significance`: importance for the history of algorithmic reasoning.
- `ethical_caution`: importance of avoiding simplified origin stories or erasure of transmission networks.
- `modern_resonance`: degree to which the theme clarifies adoption of technical systems today.

## reception_map.csv

- `reception_score`: average of the theme dimensions.
- `interpretive_status`: core, major, or supporting algorism/algebra reception thread.

## interpretation_cautions.csv

- `caution`: interpretive risk to avoid.
- `meaning`: explanation of the caution.
