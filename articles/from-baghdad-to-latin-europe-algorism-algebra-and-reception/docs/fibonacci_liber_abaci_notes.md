# Fibonacci and Liber Abaci Notes

Fibonacci should be treated as a major popularizer and teacher, not as a single-handed origin point.

Important reception layers:

- practical arithmetic;
- merchant examples;
- exchange rates;
- weights and measures;
- partnership;
- profit and loss;
- pedagogy through worked problems;
- demonstration of Hindu-Arabic numerals in use.

The importance of Liber Abaci lies in pedagogy, practical credibility, and normalization of written calculation.
