# Representation Governance Checklist

- Define what domain, system, or problem is being represented.
- Identify the intended computational task.
- Choose a representation shape that fits the task.
- Define schemas, types, constraints, keys, and invariants.
- Record what information is omitted, compressed, simplified, or aggregated.
- Test whether the representation supports the intended procedures.
- Preserve metadata, provenance, timestamps, versions, assumptions, and transformations.
- Review interpretability and contestability.
- Audit who or what becomes invisible, misclassified, flattened, or over-simplified.
- Govern schema drift, category changes, model updates, and downstream reuse.
