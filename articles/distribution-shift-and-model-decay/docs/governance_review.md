# Governance Review

Drift and decay governance should include:

- approved deployment scope;
- baseline distributions and monitoring records;
- alert thresholds;
- calibration and subgroup monitoring;
- override and incident logs;
- data-lineage review before retraining;
- rollback and suspension procedures;
- use-case expansion review;
- model versioning and change logs;
- retirement or sunset policies.

A model that cannot be monitored, paused, rolled back, or retired is not ready for accountable deployment.
