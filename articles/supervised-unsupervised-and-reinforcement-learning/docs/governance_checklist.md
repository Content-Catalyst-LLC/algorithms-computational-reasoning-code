# Governance Checklist

- Are supervised labels valid, consistent, and appropriate for the decision context?
- Was train/test separation preserved?
- Are thresholds justified in terms of consequences, not only accuracy?
- Are unsupervised clusters validated before being interpreted?
- Are feature scales and distance assumptions documented?
- Does the reinforcement-learning reward signal reflect the real purpose?
- Are exploration and policy updates safe in the intended setting?
- Can affected people challenge automated outputs?
- Are use boundaries documented clearly?
