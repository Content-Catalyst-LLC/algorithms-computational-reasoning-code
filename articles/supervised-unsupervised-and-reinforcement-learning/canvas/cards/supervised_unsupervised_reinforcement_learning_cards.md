# Canvas Cards

## Supervised Learning

- Evidence structure: labeled examples.
- Output: classification, prediction, ranking, or score.
- Risk: labels may encode measurement error or institutional history.
- Review question: what does the label actually measure?

## Unsupervised Learning

- Evidence structure: unlabeled observations.
- Output: clusters, dimensions, embeddings, or structure summaries.
- Risk: discovered structure may be overinterpreted.
- Review question: what validates the interpretation of a cluster?

## Reinforcement Learning

- Evidence structure: actions, rewards, and feedback.
- Output: policy or action rule.
- Risk: reward hacking or unsafe exploration.
- Review question: does the reward reflect the real purpose?
