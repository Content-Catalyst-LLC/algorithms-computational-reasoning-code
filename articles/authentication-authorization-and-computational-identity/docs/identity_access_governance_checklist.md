# Identity and Access Governance Checklist

Use this checklist when reviewing authentication, authorization, and computational identity systems.

1. Inventory human, service, device, API, workload, and agent identities.
2. Classify protected resources by sensitivity, owner, purpose, and retention obligations.
3. Map actions such as read, write, delete, export, approve, delegate, and administer.
4. Verify authentication strength, MFA design, recovery flows, and credential storage.
5. Review authorization granularity and object-level enforcement.
6. Apply least privilege and remove standing privileges where possible.
7. Scope sessions, tokens, claims, audiences, and expiration correctly.
8. Review machine identities and service accounts for owner, scope, rotation, and retirement.
9. Test privilege escalation and trust-boundary enforcement.
10. Preserve audit logs with actor, action, resource, context, result, and timestamp.
11. Review privacy, accessibility, exclusion, and recovery impacts.
12. Assign governance ownership and repeat lifecycle access reviews.
