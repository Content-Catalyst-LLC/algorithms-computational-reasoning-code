# Use Boundary

This repository is for educational and article-companion use.

Do not use these scripts to make real eligibility, credit, employment, health, public-service, or legal decisions. The generated data are synthetic. The decision rules are simplified. The counterfactuals are designed for teaching and audit demonstration only.

A production counterfactual reasoning system would require:

- domain-specific evidence;
- legal review;
- causal analysis;
- accessibility review;
- stakeholder and affected-person input;
- model documentation;
- appeal and correction pathways;
- continuous monitoring after deployment.
