# Method Notes

This folder treats algorithmic philosophy as practical review.

The core method is:

1. Define the real-world question.
2. Identify the formalization.
3. Document representation choices.
4. Examine abstraction losses.
5. Analyze delegation level.
6. Review explanation needs.
7. Establish contestability.
8. Assign institutional responsibility.

The goal is to ask what algorithms make possible, what they omit, what they delegate, what they explain, what they cannot decide, and how their consequences are governed.
