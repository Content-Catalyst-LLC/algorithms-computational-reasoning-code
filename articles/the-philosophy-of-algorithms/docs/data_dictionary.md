# Data Dictionary

## philosophical_domains.csv

- `domain_id`: algorithmic domain or deployment form.
- `formalization_intensity`: how strongly the domain turns messy questions into formal procedures.
- `abstraction_risk`: risk that abstraction omits important context.
- `representation_risk`: risk that data, labels, features, embeddings, or records distort reality.
- `delegation_level`: degree to which practical authority is delegated to algorithmic procedure.
- `opacity`: difficulty of explaining how output was produced.
- `optimization_pressure`: degree to which metrics/objectives may reshape behavior.
- `contestability_need`: need for appeal, correction, and affected-person recourse.
- `institutional_consequence`: likely consequence for rights, access, attention, opportunity, risk, or resources.
- `human_judgment_requirement`: need for genuine human authority and review.
- `governance_urgency`: need for documentation, monitoring, audit, oversight, and repair.

## algorithmic_philosophy_review_map.csv

- `review_score`: average of philosophical review dimensions.
- `review_status`: priority category for philosophical review.
