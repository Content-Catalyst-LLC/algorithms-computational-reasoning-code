# Use Boundaries

This companion folder is educational. It is not legal advice, compliance certification, audit certification, medical advice, credit advice, public-benefits advice, employment advice, civil-rights advice, or operational guidance.

Do not use the synthetic human review scores here to certify real systems, approve deployment, or claim regulatory compliance.

Before using human-in-the-loop metrics in practice, require:

- legal and governance review;
- domain expertise;
- human factors expertise;
- reviewer and affected-person input where appropriate;
- privacy and safety review;
- validated workflow studies;
- contestability and remediation pathways;
- independent audit where warranted.
