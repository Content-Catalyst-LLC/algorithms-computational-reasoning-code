# Reviewer Workflow Notes

Human-in-the-loop systems should be evaluated by what reviewers can actually do.

Review workflow questions:

- Can reviewers see the relevant evidence?
- Can reviewers see uncertainty and unsupported-use warnings?
- Can reviewers override or escalate without unreasonable friction?
- Are reviewers trained to recognize automation bias and model limits?
- Are reviewers given enough time for the stakes of the decision?
- Are override and escalation patterns documented?
- Are reviewers protected from speed-only or conformity incentives?
- Do appeals and corrections feed back into system improvement?

A final human click is not meaningful judgment unless the workflow supports judgment.
