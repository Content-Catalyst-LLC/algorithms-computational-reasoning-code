# Governance Review

Human-in-the-loop governance should include:

- role-specific review protocol;
- evidence access requirements;
- uncertainty and limitation display rules;
- override and escalation authority;
- workload and staffing review;
- reviewer training records;
- override and escalation logs;
- appeal and correction pathways;
- remediation and system-learning duties;
- institutional ownership for review conditions.

Governance should prevent human oversight from becoming symbolic.
