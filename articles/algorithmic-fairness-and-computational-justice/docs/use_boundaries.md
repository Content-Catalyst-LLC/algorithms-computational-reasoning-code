# Use Boundaries

This companion folder is educational. It is not legal, civil-rights, employment, credit, health, public-benefits, housing, insurance, platform-governance, or compliance advice.

Do not use the synthetic fairness scores here to certify real systems, approve deployment, make hiring or lending decisions, or claim legal compliance.

Before using fairness metrics in practice, require:

- legal and civil-rights review;
- domain expertise;
- affected-person and stakeholder consultation;
- validated data and measurement review;
- documented metric choices and tradeoffs;
- contestability and remediation pathways;
- monitoring and independent audit where appropriate.
