# Method Notes

This folder treats fairness as a lifecycle and governance responsibility.

The core method is:

1. Define the decision, stakes, affected people, and comparison groups.
2. Review labels, features, proxies, and measurement validity.
3. Choose fairness definitions and document tradeoffs.
4. Compute selection, error-rate, calibration, and justice-capacity metrics.
5. Review individual and procedural fairness.
6. Monitor deployment, drift, appeals, incidents, and subgroup outcomes.
7. Connect findings to remediation and governance change.

The examples are dependency-light and educational.
