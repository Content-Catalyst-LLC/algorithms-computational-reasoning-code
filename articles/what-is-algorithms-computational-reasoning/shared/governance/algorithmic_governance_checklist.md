# Algorithmic Governance Checklist

- Define the real-world concern separately from the computational task.
- Document inputs, outputs, assumptions, data provenance, and representation choices.
- State correctness evidence, tests, and known failure modes.
- Estimate time, memory, maintenance, and monitoring costs.
- Identify affected stakeholders and the cost of false positives and false negatives.
- Document whether outputs are advisory, decision-supporting, or decision-making.
- Provide review, appeal, incident response, and retirement conditions.
