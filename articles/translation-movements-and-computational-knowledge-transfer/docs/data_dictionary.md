# Data Dictionary

## transfer_themes.csv

- `theme_id`: interpretive theme in computational knowledge transfer.
- `procedural_fidelity`: importance of preserving operations, steps, examples, and method usability.
- `vocabulary_mapping`: importance of technical terms, glosses, borrowing, calques, and conceptual mapping.
- `diagram_table_preservation`: importance of nonverbal knowledge such as diagrams, tables, labels, units, and layouts.
- `institutional_support`: importance of patrons, libraries, scribes, schools, instruments, and teaching environments.
- `error_control`: importance of collation, comparison, correction, commentary, and testing.
- `adaptation`: importance of fitting a procedure to new contexts, instruments, calendars, units, or questions.
- `historical_significance`: importance for the history of algorithmic reasoning.
- `ethical_caution`: importance of avoiding simplified origin stories or erasure of intermediaries.
- `modern_resonance`: degree to which the theme clarifies documentation, interoperability, versioning, and technical transfer today.

## transfer_map.csv

- `transfer_score`: average of the theme dimensions.
- `interpretive_status`: core, major, or supporting computational knowledge transfer thread.

## interpretation_cautions.csv

- `caution`: interpretive risk to avoid.
- `meaning`: explanation of the caution.
