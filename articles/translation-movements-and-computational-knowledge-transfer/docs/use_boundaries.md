# Use Boundaries

This companion folder is educational and interpretive.

Use these files to support:

- teaching translation movements as knowledge infrastructure;
- conceptual mapping of technical transfer;
- analysis of diagrams, tables, vocabulary, and commentary;
- structured comparison of multilingual relay systems;
- computational scaffolds for humanities-oriented analysis.

Do not treat the synthetic scores as manuscript facts or as a replacement for specialist philological, codicological, paleographic, or historical analysis.
