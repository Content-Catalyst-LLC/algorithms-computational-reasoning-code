# Governance Checklist: Multi-Objective Optimization and Trade-Off Reasoning

- Are all objectives listed and justified?
- Are metrics valid proxies for the criteria they represent?
- Are weights, thresholds, and priorities documented?
- Are non-negotiable values modeled as constraints rather than weak penalties?
- Were dominated alternatives identified and removed from serious consideration?
- Was a Pareto frontier or efficient alternative set reviewed?
- Were sensitivity, scenario, and robustness tests run?
- Were fairness and distributional effects reviewed?
- Were stakeholder perspectives included?
- Is the final compromise documented with rationale and limitations?
