# FLOW-MATIC and COBOL Notes

FLOW-MATIC and COBOL matter because they brought programming closer to business procedure.

Important themes:

- English-like syntax;
- file and record processing;
- business vocabulary;
- institutional data workflows;
- portability across machines;
- standards and maintainability;
- long-lived enterprise systems.

COBOL's longevity shows that programming languages are institutional infrastructures, not just syntactic designs.
