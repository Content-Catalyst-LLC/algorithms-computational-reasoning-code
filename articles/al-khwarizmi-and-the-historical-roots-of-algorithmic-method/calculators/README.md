# Calculators

These small calculators support article concepts without external packages. They are educational conceptual scaffolds, not historical proof tools.

## Python calculators

- `method_score_calculator.py` — average al-Khwārizmī method dimensions.
- `place_value_calculator.py` — evaluate a number from digit-position pairs.
- `carrying_calculator.py` — show a simple carrying operation.
- `equation_case_classifier.py` — classify simple square/root/number equation forms.
- `restoration_balancing_calculator.py` — normalize simple left/right term counts.
- `solution_verification_calculator.py` — verify a candidate for x^2 + bx = c.
- `etymology_scope_calculator.py` — distinguish name, algorism, algorithm, formal algorithm, program, and system.
- `myth_caution_calculator.py` — flag overclaims about al-Khwārizmī.

## R calculators

- `khwarizmi_method_score.R` — compute al-Khwārizmī method score.
