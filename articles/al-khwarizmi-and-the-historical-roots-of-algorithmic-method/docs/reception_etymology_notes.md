# Reception and Etymology Notes

The word algorithm preserves historical memory of al-Khwārizmī through Latinized forms of his name and the tradition of algorism.

Required distinctions:

- al-Khwārizmī: historical scholar;
- algorism: written arithmetic using Hindu-Arabic numerals;
- algorithm: later broader term for computational method;
- formal algorithm: modern finite effective procedure;
- program: machine-executable implementation;
- algorithmic system: procedure, data, software, institution, and governance.

Word history matters, but it is not the whole history.
