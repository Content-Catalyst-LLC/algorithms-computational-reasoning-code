# Algebraic Method Notes

Al-Khwārizmī's algebraic method is verbal, case-based, and procedural.

Important features:

- unknown quantity;
- root;
- square;
- number;
- equation case;
- rule;
- restoration;
- balancing;
- completion;
- extraction;
- verification.

The method is not modern symbolic algebra, but it is structured rule-governed problem solving.
