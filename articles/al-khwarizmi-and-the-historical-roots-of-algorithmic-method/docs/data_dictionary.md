# Data Dictionary

## khwarizmi_themes.csv

- `theme_id`: interpretive theme in the al-Khwārizmī method map.
- `arithmetic_method`: importance of written arithmetic, algorism, numerals, place value, carrying, borrowing, and checking.
- `algebraic_procedure`: importance of cases, unknowns, roots, squares, numbers, al-jabr, al-muqābalah, and solution rules.
- `representation`: importance of numerals, words, tables, diagrams, and forms of expression.
- `transformation`: importance of state-changing operations such as restoration, balancing, completion, and reduction.
- `proof_relation`: importance of correctness, demonstration, worked examples, or verification.
- `transmission`: importance of Arabic writing, translation, Latin reception, teaching, and institutional memory.
- `etymology`: importance of name history, algorism, and algorithm.
- `institutional_adoption`: importance of schools, commerce, scholarship, and practical use.
- `historiographic_caution`: importance of avoiding myth, anachronism, and etymology-only explanation.
- `modern_resonance`: degree to which the theme clarifies algorithmic reasoning today.

## khwarizmi_algorithmic_method_map.csv

- `method_score`: average of the theme dimensions.
- `interpretive_status`: core, major, or supporting al-Khwārizmī algorithmic-method thread.

## interpretation_cautions.csv

- `caution`: interpretive risk to avoid.
- `meaning`: explanation of the caution.
