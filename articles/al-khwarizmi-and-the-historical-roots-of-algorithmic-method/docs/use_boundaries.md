# Use Boundaries

This companion folder is educational and interpretive.

Use these files to support:

- teaching al-Khwārizmī's role in algorithmic method;
- distinguishing algorism from algorithm;
- explaining arithmetic, algebra, tables, translation, and reception;
- evaluating origin-story claims;
- creating public-facing historical explanations.

Do not treat the synthetic scores as historical facts or as a replacement for specialist historical, philological, codicological, mathematical, or manuscript scholarship.
