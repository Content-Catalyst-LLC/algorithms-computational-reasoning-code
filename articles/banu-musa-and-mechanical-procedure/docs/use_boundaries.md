# Use Boundaries

This companion folder is educational and interpretive. It is not engineering, construction, safety, or operational guidance.

Use these files to support:

- teaching historical mechanical reasoning;
- conceptual mapping;
- analysis of procedure in material systems;
- structured comparison of mechanical themes;
- computational scaffolds for humanities-oriented analysis.

Do not use the calculators or examples to build, operate, or certify real hydraulic, pneumatic, mechanical, safety-critical, or pressure-based devices.
