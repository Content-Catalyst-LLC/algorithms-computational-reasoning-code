# Automation and Anachronism Notes

Banū Mūsā devices should not be called modern robots, computers, or AI systems.

Better terms:

- mechanical device;
- automaton;
- hydraulic or pneumatic device;
- embodied procedure;
- mechanical control;
- automatic behavior;
- procedural mechanics.

The devices belong to the long history of automation because they delegate action to structure, not because they are modern digital machines.
