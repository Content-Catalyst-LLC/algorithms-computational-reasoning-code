# Due Process Notes

Contestability is not the same as transparency.

A contestable system should support:

- notice that a decision occurred and automation was involved;
- understandable reasons tied to the specific decision;
- access to relevant evidence or data;
- a practical appeal channel;
- meaningful human review;
- correction of decision and underlying records;
- remedy when harm occurred;
- audit trails and institutional accountability.

A low appeal rate does not prove fairness. It may indicate lack of notice, high burden, fear, complexity, language barriers, or low expectation of success.
