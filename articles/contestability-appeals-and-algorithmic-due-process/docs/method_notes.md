# Method Notes

This folder treats algorithmic due process as a reviewable system.

The core method is:

1. Classify decision stakes and consequences.
2. Document algorithmic involvement.
3. Evaluate notice, reasons, and evidence access.
4. Evaluate appeal access, burden, and timeliness.
5. Evaluate human review authority and independence.
6. Evaluate correction, remedy, and downstream propagation.
7. Preserve audit trails and monitor appeal outcomes over time.

The examples are dependency-light and educational.
