# Ranking Governance Checklist

- Candidate sources are documented.
- Filters are named and reviewable.
- Ranking signals are described.
- Score construction is traceable.
- Personalization is disclosed where appropriate.
- Diversity, novelty, and exposure are evaluated.
- Feedback loops are monitored.
- Fairness and distributional effects are reviewed.
- Users have explanation or appeal paths where decisions are consequential.
- Model, data, and policy versions are recorded.
