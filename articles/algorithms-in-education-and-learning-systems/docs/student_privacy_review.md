# Student Privacy Review

Student privacy review should include:

- data minimization;
- purpose limitation;
- clear notice;
- retention and deletion rules;
- vendor access and contracting;
- security controls;
- access logs;
- secondary-use limits;
- student and family rights where relevant;
- safeguards for sensitive learning records, disability accommodations, disciplinary records, financial aid, platform traces, writing samples, voice, video, and advising notes.

Student data should be governed as part of educational care, not merely institutional administration.
