# Equity and Accessibility Review

Educational equity and accessibility review should examine:

- digital access and broadband barriers;
- device access;
- disability accommodations;
- multilingual learners;
- neurodivergent learners;
- adult learners;
- working students;
- caregivers;
- rural learners;
- subgroup error;
- tracking risk;
- prior opportunity bias;
- discipline record bias;
- student voice and contestability.

Education algorithms should expand opportunity and access, not automate inherited inequality.
