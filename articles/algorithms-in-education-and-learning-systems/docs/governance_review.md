# Governance Review

Learning-system governance should include:

- algorithm inventory;
- pedagogical review;
- educational equity review;
- student privacy review;
- accessibility review;
- procurement and vendor accountability;
- human review and teacher override;
- student contestability;
- monitoring;
- audit trails;
- incident response;
- stop, redesign, remediation, and retirement authority.

Educational governance should make algorithmic systems visible before they shape student experience.
