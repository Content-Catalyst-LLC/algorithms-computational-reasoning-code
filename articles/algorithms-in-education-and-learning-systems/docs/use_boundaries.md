# Use Boundaries

This companion folder is educational. It is not legal advice, educational policy advice, admissions advice, assessment validation certification, accessibility certification, privacy compliance certification, academic-integrity adjudication, or operational guidance.

Do not use the synthetic scores here to approve real education systems, automate admissions, deny students support, discipline students, score high-stakes work, certify accessibility, or claim privacy compliance.

Before using learning-system metrics in practice, require:

- qualified educators and learning scientists;
- student privacy and legal review;
- accessibility review;
- educational equity review;
- human review and contestability;
- independent validation where warranted;
- monitoring, remediation, and stop authority.
