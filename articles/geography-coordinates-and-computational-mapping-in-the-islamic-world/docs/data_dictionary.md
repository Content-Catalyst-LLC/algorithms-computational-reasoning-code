# Data Dictionary

## computational_mapping_themes.csv

- `theme_id`: interpretive theme in computational mapping.
- `spatial_representation`: importance of mapping, visual reasoning, route structure, or spatial model.
- `coordinate_structure`: importance of coordinates, reference frames, mathematical location, or positional records.
- `procedural_clarity`: degree to which the theme involves ordered spatial procedure.
- `institutional_use`: importance in trade, administration, pilgrimage, scholarship, navigation, or governance.
- `correction_awareness`: importance of data correction, source comparison, projection choice, or validation.
- `transmission_importance`: importance of copying, translation, redrawing, commentary, teaching, or localization.
- `modern_resonance`: degree to which the theme clarifies modern geospatial computation.

## computational_mapping_map.csv

- `mapping_score`: average of the theme dimensions.
- `interpretive_status`: core, major, or supporting computational mapping thread.

## interpretation_cautions.csv

- `caution`: interpretive risk to avoid.
- `meaning`: explanation of the caution.

## computational_mapping_summary.csv

- `themes_reviewed`: number of mapping themes reviewed.
- `core_threads`: themes marked as core computational mapping threads.
- `major_threads`: themes marked as major computational mapping threads.
- `supporting_threads`: themes marked as supporting computational mapping threads.
- `mean_mapping_score`: mean interpretive mapping score.
