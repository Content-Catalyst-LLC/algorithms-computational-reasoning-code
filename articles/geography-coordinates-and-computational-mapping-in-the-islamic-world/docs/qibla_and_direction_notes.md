# Qibla and Directional Calculation Notes

Directional calculation transforms a local position into an orientation toward a reference place.

Important interpretive points:

- Direction depends on location and reference.
- Approximate and exact methods can differ.
- Instruments, tables, astronomy, and geometry may all be involved.
- Religious, astronomical, and geographical practices can overlap.
- Educational examples here are conceptual only and not religious guidance.

Directional calculation is a spatial algorithm: given a place, produce an orientation.
