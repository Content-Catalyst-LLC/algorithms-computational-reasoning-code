# Governance Review

Transparency, explainability, and interpretability governance should include:

- audience and use-case definition;
- model and data documentation;
- explanation method selection;
- faithfulness and stability testing;
- uncertainty and limitation disclosure;
- decision logs and audit trails;
- contestability and appeal pathways;
- remediation and recurrence prevention;
- named owners for explanation quality and documentation maintenance.

A system should not be described as explainable unless explanations support meaningful review and action.
