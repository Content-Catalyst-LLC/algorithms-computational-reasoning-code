# Use Boundaries

This companion folder is educational. It is not legal advice, compliance certification, audit certification, model-risk approval, civil-rights advice, medical advice, credit advice, public-benefits advice, employment advice, or operational guidance.

Do not use the synthetic explanation scores here to certify real systems, approve deployment, or claim regulatory compliance.

Before using explanation metrics in practice, require:

- legal and governance review;
- domain expertise;
- affected-person and stakeholder input where appropriate;
- validated explanation methods;
- testing for faithfulness and stability;
- documented uncertainty and limitations;
- contestability and remediation pathways;
- independent audit where warranted.
