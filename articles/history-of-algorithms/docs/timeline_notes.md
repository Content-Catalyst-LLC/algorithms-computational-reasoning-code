# Timeline Notes

The article's timeline is interpretive rather than exhaustive.

Major layers:

1. Ancient worked procedures
2. Greek demonstrated procedure
3. Indian numeration and calculation
4. Chinese procedural mathematics
5. Islamic-world algebra, algorism, tables, cryptanalysis, and mechanical procedure
6. Latin reception and commercial arithmetic
7. Early modern symbolism and calculation
8. Mechanical and programmable machines
9. Logic, formal systems, and computability
10. Electronic computing and software
11. AI, platforms, and governance

The history is not a straight line. It is a layered network of representation, method, proof, machine, institution, and responsibility.
