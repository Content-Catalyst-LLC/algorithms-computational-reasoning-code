# Use Boundaries

This companion folder is educational. It is not legal advice, compliance certification, audit certification, civil-rights advice, medical advice, credit advice, public-benefits advice, employment advice, or operational guidance.

Do not use the synthetic non-use scores here to certify real systems, approve deployment, reject deployment, or claim regulatory compliance.

Before using non-use review metrics in practice, require:

- legal and governance review;
- domain expertise;
- affected-person and stakeholder input where appropriate;
- data stewardship review;
- human factors and rights review;
- validated impact assessment methods;
- contestability and remediation pathways;
- independent audit where warranted.
