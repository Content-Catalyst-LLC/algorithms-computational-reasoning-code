# Governance Review

Algorithmic non-use governance should include:

- explicit non-use option in approval workflows;
- target legitimacy review;
- data and proxy review;
- stakes and irreversibility review;
- contestability and repairability review;
- human judgment and dignity review;
- alternatives analysis;
- refusal, pause, rollback, and retirement authority;
- non-use register and conditions for reconsideration.

Governance should protect the right to say no to automation.
