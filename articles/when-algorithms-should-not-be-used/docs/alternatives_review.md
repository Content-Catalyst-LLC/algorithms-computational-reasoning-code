# Alternatives Review

Non-use does not mean doing nothing.

Common alternatives include:

- clearer policy rules;
- improved staffing and training;
- human-led review;
- participatory decision processes;
- better intake forms and service navigation;
- universal or needs-based support;
- transparent checklists;
- appeal and correction infrastructure;
- data correction before automation;
- less intrusive monitoring;
- limited information support rather than decision automation.

The best solution to a decision problem is not always a decision system.
