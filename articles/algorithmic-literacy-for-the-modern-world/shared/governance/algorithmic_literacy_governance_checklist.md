# Algorithmic Literacy Governance Checklist

- Identify where an algorithmic system shapes visibility, access, ranking, recommendation, decision support, or automation.
- Define the problem being formalized.
- Document inputs, data sources, quality, scope, and missingness.
- Explain outputs, output meaning, uncertainty, and interpretation limits.
- Name what the system optimizes, rewards, filters, or suppresses.
- Identify affected users and communities.
- Provide correction, appeal, review, or escalation paths where consequences matter.
- Monitor drift, failure, feedback loops, and unintended impacts.
- Maintain documentation, accountability, and revision processes.
- Keep human judgment active where values, uncertainty, or consequences require it.
