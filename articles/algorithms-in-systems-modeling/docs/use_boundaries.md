# Use Boundaries

This companion folder is educational. It is not legal advice, compliance certification, audit certification, civil-rights advice, public-health advice, infrastructure advice, climate advice, financial advice, engineering advice, or operational guidance.

Do not use the synthetic systems-modeling scores here to certify real systems, approve interventions, or claim regulatory compliance.

Before using systems-modeling metrics in practice, require:

- domain expertise;
- model validation and calibration review;
- data provenance review;
- sensitivity and uncertainty analysis;
- affected-person and stakeholder input where appropriate;
- legal and governance review;
- monitoring, stop rules, and accountability records;
- independent audit where warranted.
