# Governance Review

Algorithmic harm governance should include:

- harm taxonomy;
- impact and severity review;
- disaggregated outcome monitoring;
- incident reporting and escalation;
- appeal and contestability review;
- remediation and repair procedures;
- audit trails and model versioning;
- procurement and vendor accountability;
- ownership across design, data, deployment, monitoring, and repair;
- recurrence analysis.

A responsible institution does not merely explain harm. It repairs harm and changes the conditions that made it possible.
