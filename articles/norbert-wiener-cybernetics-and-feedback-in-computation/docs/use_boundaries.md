# Use Boundaries

This companion folder is educational and interpretive.

Use these files to support:

- teaching feedback and cybernetics;
- distinguishing negative and positive feedback;
- mapping loops in software, platforms, and AI systems;
- discussing control, prediction, delay, and noise;
- building simple calculators and smoke tests for article concepts;
- supporting governance conversations about automation and feedback loops.

Do not treat synthetic scores as control proofs, safety certifications, or formal governance decisions.
