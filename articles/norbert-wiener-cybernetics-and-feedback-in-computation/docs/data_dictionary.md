# Data Dictionary

## wiener_themes.csv

- `theme_id`: interpretive theme in the Wiener cybernetic feedback map.
- `feedback_centrality`: importance of feedback loops.
- `control_relevance`: importance of control, regulation, correction, and actuation.
- `communication_relevance`: importance of messages, signals, and information flow.
- `prediction_relevance`: relevance to anticipation, forecasting, and future state estimation.
- `stability_relevance`: relevance to negative feedback, damping, robustness, and homeostasis.
- `amplification_risk`: relevance to positive feedback, runaway dynamics, and cascade risk.
- `automation_ethics`: relevance to human agency, labor, dignity, and responsibility.
- `ai_relevance`: relevance to AI systems, reinforcement learning, platforms, and adaptive models.
- `institutional_relevance`: relevance to organizations, governance, incentives, and power.
- `governance_caution`: relevance to accountability, oversight, auditing, and safe deployment.

## wiener_cybernetic_feedback_map.csv

- `cybernetic_score`: average of the theme dimensions.
- `interpretive_status`: core, major, or supporting Wiener cybernetic-feedback thread.

## interpretation_cautions.csv

- `caution`: interpretive risk to avoid.
- `meaning`: explanation of the caution.
