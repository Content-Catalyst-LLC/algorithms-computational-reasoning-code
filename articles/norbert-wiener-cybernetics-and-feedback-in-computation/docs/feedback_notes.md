# Feedback Notes

Feedback is a structural relation, not merely a response.

Negative feedback:

- reduces deviation from a target;
- supports stability;
- can fail under delay, noise, or overcorrection.

Positive feedback:

- amplifies change;
- can support growth and learning;
- can also produce runaway dynamics, lock-in, cascades, and institutional harm.

Feedback must be designed, monitored, and governed.
