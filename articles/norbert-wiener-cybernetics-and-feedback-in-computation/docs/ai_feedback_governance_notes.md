# AI Feedback Governance Notes

AI systems often operate in feedback loops.

Governance should examine:

- model outputs that change user behavior;
- recommendations that change future training data;
- rankings that reshape incentives;
- predictions that become self-fulfilling;
- metrics that get gamed;
- harms that are hidden by aggregate performance;
- rollback, appeal, override, and contestability mechanisms.

Model governance without loop governance is incomplete.
