# Inheritance Allocation Notes

Inheritance calculation is rule-governed allocation.

A procedural analysis should identify:

1. the estate or total;
2. eligible categories;
3. shares or weights;
4. exclusions and adjustments;
5. common denominator or common measure;
6. distribution;
7. remainder handling;
8. total check.

This folder provides educational calculators for allocation patterns. It does not provide legal advice.
