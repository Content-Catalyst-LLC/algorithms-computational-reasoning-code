# Data Dictionary

## practical_calculation_themes.csv

- `theme_id`: interpretive theme in practical calculation.
- `procedure`: degree to which the theme involves ordered repeatable calculation.
- `representation`: importance of quantities, units, shares, diagrams, tables, or ledgers.
- `institutional_importance`: degree to which the calculation affects trade, law, property, administration, or education.
- `verification`: importance of checking, recomputation, records, dispute resolution, and auditability.
- `transmission`: importance of teaching, copying, manuals, examples, and institutional movement.
- `modern_resonance`: degree to which the theme clarifies modern algorithmic systems.

## practical_calculation_procedure_map.csv

- `practical_score`: average of the theme dimensions.
- `interpretive_status`: core, major, or supporting practical calculation thread.

## interpretation_cautions.csv

- `caution`: interpretive risk to avoid.
- `meaning`: explanation of the caution.

## practical_calculation_summary.csv

- `themes_reviewed`: number of practical calculation themes reviewed.
- `core_threads`: themes marked as core practical calculation threads.
- `major_threads`: themes marked as major practical calculation threads.
- `supporting_threads`: themes marked as supporting practical calculation threads.
- `mean_practical_score`: mean interpretive practical calculation score.
