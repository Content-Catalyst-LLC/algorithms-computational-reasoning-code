# Methodology Notes

This companion treats the halting problem as a practical governance boundary for automation claims.

Audit dimensions:
- program-scope clarity
- termination-claim clarity
- undecidability awareness
- bounded-analysis honesty
- unknown-status handling
- timeout policy
- false-certainty risk control
- human-review pathway
- traceability
- governance readiness

Scores are educational diagnostics, not universal measurements.
