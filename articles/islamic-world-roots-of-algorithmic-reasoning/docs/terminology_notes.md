# Terminology Notes

## Algorithm

The modern word algorithm is connected through Latin forms of al-Khwārizmī's name. This is strong evidence of transmission history, but it should not be used to claim that all procedural reasoning began with one person.

## Algebra

The word algebra is tied to al-jabr in al-Khwārizmī's algebraic treatise. The historical practice was often verbal and procedural before modern symbolic notation.

## Algorism

Algorism referred to calculation with Hindu-Arabic numerals in medieval Latin traditions. It is important for understanding the transmission of written positional calculation.

## Procedure

Procedure is the central bridge term. It lets us discuss algorithms before modern code without projecting modern computers backward.
