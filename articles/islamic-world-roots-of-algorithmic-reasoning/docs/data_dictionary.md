# Data Dictionary

## historical_themes.csv

- `theme_id`: interpretive theme in Islamic-world algorithmic reasoning.
- `procedural_explicitness`: degree to which the theme involves clear stepwise procedure.
- `transmission_importance`: importance in translation, teaching, adaptation, or later reception.
- `practical_application`: degree of practical use in institutions, commerce, law, astronomy, geography, craft, or administration.
- `representation_importance`: importance of notation, tables, language, diagrams, instruments, or material form.
- `modern_resonance`: degree to which the theme clarifies modern computational reasoning.

## historical_algorithmic_reasoning_map.csv

- `significance_score`: average of the theme dimensions.
- `interpretive_status`: core, major, or supporting algorithmic-reasoning thread.

## historical_cautions.csv

- `caution`: interpretive risk to avoid.
- `meaning`: explanation of the caution.

## historical_reasoning_summary.csv

- `themes_reviewed`: number of historical themes reviewed.
- `core_threads`: themes marked as core algorithmic-reasoning threads.
- `major_threads`: themes marked as major algorithmic-reasoning threads.
- `supporting_threads`: themes marked as supporting algorithmic-reasoning threads.
- `mean_significance_score`: mean interpretive significance score.
